# wybmvalentine

valentines? <br>
a webpage that asks someone to be your valentine! <br>
built by mansha (me!)

<b> how it works </b>

there are two pages: <br>
index.html - the ask. opens with a heart graph and the question. <br>
yes.html - the confirmation. shown to the sender after their person says yes. <br>

<b> sending it to someone </b>

click the envelope icon -> type their name and an optional note -> copy the link <br>
the link will look like: https://wybmvalentine.vercel.app/?to=name&msg=note <br>
when they open it, the question is personalised with their name and a little letter appears from the envelope. after they say yes, they can copy the link back to you confirming it.
