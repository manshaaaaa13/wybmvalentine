# wybmvalentine

valentines?
a webpage that asks someone to be your valentine!
built by mansha (me!)

<b> how it works </b>

there are two pages:
index.html - the ask. opens with a heart graph and the question.
yes.html - the confirmation. shown to the sender after their person says yes.

<b> sending it to someone </b>
click the envelope icon -> type their name and an optional note -> copy the link
the link will look like: https://wybmvalentine.vercel.app/?to=name&msg=note
when they open it, the question is personalised with their name and a little letter appears from the envelope. after they say yes, they can copy the link back to you confirming it.
